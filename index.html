marbleo.us

an informatiCup 2011 project

Share your Map!
  • Drag blocks from the palette onto the map.
  • You can click a block to rotate it.
  • Click Share your Map! to send your friends what you have built.

Marbleo.us was build by Simon Hohberg and Robert Böhnke for the Gesellschaft für Informatik informatiCup 2011

Feel free to browse through the documentation

Marbleo.us is an ongoing project and far from done. We hope that soon you will be able to take a marble for a ride on your creation

This project was built using CoffeeScript
